Account Issues

How do I create an account?

To create an account on Thanh Lý Sinh Viên:

  1. Click on the "Account" button in the top navigation bar
  2. Select "Sign Up" from the dropdown menu
  3. Enter your university email address (must end with .edu.vn)
  4. Create a strong password
  5. Fill in your personal information
  6. Verify your email address by clicking the link sent to your inbox
  7. Complete your profile by adding a profile picture and additional information

Note: You must have a valid university email address to register.

How do I verify my student status?

To verify your student status:

  1. Log in to your account
  2. Go to "Account Settings" > "Verification"
  3. Upload a clear photo of your student ID card (front and back)
  4. Alternatively, you can upload a screenshot of your student portal or a recent enrollment confirmation
  5. Our team will review your submission within 24-48 hours
  6. You'll receive an email notification once your student status is verified

Verification gives you a "Verified Student" badge on your profile, which helps build trust with other users.

I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

If you've forgotten your password:

  1. Click on "Account" in the top navigation bar
  2. Select "Log In" from the dropdown menu
  3. Click on "Forgot Password?" below the login form
  4. Enter the email address associated with your account
  5. Check your email for a password reset link
  6. Click the link and follow the instructions to create a new password

The password reset link is valid for 24 hours. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or request another reset link.

How do I change my profile information?

To update your profile information:

  1. Log in to your account
  2. Click on your profile icon in the top right corner
  3. Select "Profile Settings" from the dropdown menu
  4. Edit your personal information, contact details, or profile picture
  5. Click "Save Changes" to update your profile

Note: Some information, such as your university email, cannot be changed after registration. If you need to update this information, please contact our support team.

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account:

  1. Log in to your account
  2. Go to "Account Settings" > "Privacy & Security"
  3. Scroll down to the bottom and click "Delete Account"
  4. Read the information about account deletion carefully
  5. Enter your password to confirm
  6. Click "Permanently Delete My Account"

Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your listings, messages, and transaction history will be permanently removed.

If you have active listings or ongoing transactions, we recommend resolving these before deleting your account.

Buying

How do I contact a seller?

To contact a seller about an item:

  1. Navigate to the item listing page
  2. Click the "Contact Seller" button below the item description
  3. Type your message in the chat window that appears
  4. Click "Send" to initiate a conversation with the seller

You can also access all your conversations by clicking on "Messages" in your account dashboard.

Tip: Be specific in your initial message. Ask about the item's condition, availability, or any other details that aren't clear from the listing.

How do I make an offer on an item?

To make an offer on an item:

  1. Navigate to the item listing page
  2. Click the "Make Offer" button (if the seller has enabled this option)
  3. Enter your offer amount in the dialog box
  4. Add a message to the seller (optional)
  5. Click "Submit Offer"

The seller will receive a notification about your offer and can choose to accept, decline, or counter it. You'll be notified of their response.

Tip: Make reasonable offers based on the item's condition and market value. Extremely low offers may be automatically rejected or ignored by sellers.

How do I arrange a meetup with a seller?

To arrange a meetup with a seller:

  1. Discuss and agree on a price through the messaging system
  2. Suggest a few possible meeting times and locations
  3. Choose a safe, public location on or near campus (e.g., library, student union, campus coffee shop)
  4. Confirm the meeting details before heading out

For your safety:

  • Always meet in public, well-lit areas during daylight hours when possible
  • Consider bringing a friend to the meetup
  • Inform someone else about your meeting plans
  • Check the item thoroughly before completing the purchase

See our Safety Tips page for more information on safe trading practices.

What payment methods can I use?

Thanh Lý Sinh Viên supports the following payment methods:

  • Cash (in-person): The most common method for campus meetups
  • Bank Transfer: Direct transfers between Vietnamese banks
  • Mobile Payment Apps: MoMo, ZaloPay, VNPay
  • Campus Cash: If your university supports digital campus payment systems

Important: For your protection, we recommend:

  • Using cash for in-person transactions
  • Never sending money in advance before receiving an item
  • Being cautious of sellers who only accept unusual payment methods
  • Keeping a record of your payment (receipt, transfer confirmation, etc.)

All payments are arranged directly between buyers and sellers. Thanh Lý Sinh Viên does not process payments or hold funds.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Is Thanh Lý Sinh Viên only for students?

Yes, our platform is exclusively for university students in Vietnam. We verify student status through university email addresses and student ID verification.

Is there a fee to sell items?

No, listing and selling items on Thanh Lý Sinh Viên is completely free. We do not charge any commission or listing fees.

How long do listings stay active?

Listings remain active for 30 days. After that, you can easily renew them from your seller dashboard if the item is still available.

Can I sell textbooks from any university?

Yes, you can sell textbooks from any university or course. We recommend including details about the course code and university to help buyers find relevant materials.

How do I report suspicious users?

You can report suspicious users by clicking the "Report" button on their profile or listing. Our team reviews all reports within 24 hours.

Can I delete messages with a seller/buyer?

Yes, you can delete conversation threads from your messages inbox. However, the other user will still have access to the conversation history.
