Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about using our marketplace

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is simple:

1. Click on the "Account" button in the top navigation bar

2. Select "Sign Up" and enter your university email address

3. Fill in your personal details and create a password

4. Verify your email address by clicking the link sent to your inbox

5. Complete your profile by adding a profile picture and additional information

Note: You must use your university email address to register, as this helps us maintain a trusted community of students.

How do I reset my password?

If you've forgotten your password, follow these steps:

1. Click on the "Account" button in the navigation bar

2. Select "Log In" and then click "Forgot Password"

3. Enter your university email address

4. Check your email for a password reset link

5. Click the link and follow the instructions to create a new password

If you don't receive the email within a few minutes, check your spam folder or contact our support team.

How do I contact a seller?

To contact a seller about an item:

1. Navigate to the item listing you're interested in

2. Click the "Contact Seller" button on the listing

3. This will open our secure messaging system where you can ask questions or arrange a meeting

All communication should happen through our platform for safety and to maintain a record of your conversation.

Can I negotiate the price?

Yes, price negotiation is common on our platform. When contacting a seller, you can politely offer a different price than what's listed.

Tips for successful negotiation:

- Be respectful and reasonable with your offers

- Explain why you're offering a different price

- Be prepared for the seller to counter-offer or decline

- Once you agree on a price, stick to it when you meet in person

Where should I meet the seller?

For your safety, we recommend meeting in public places on campus during daylight hours:

- The student union building

- Campus libraries

- Busy cafeterias or coffee shops

- Residence hall lobbies

Many campuses also have designated "safe exchange zones" that may be monitored by security cameras. Never meet at private residences or isolated locations, especially for first-time meetings.

See our Safety Tips page for more information.

How do I list an item for sale?

To create a listing:

1. Click the "Sell Item" button in the navigation bar

2. Upload clear photos of your item (at least 2-3 images from different angles)

3. Create a descriptive title

4. Select the appropriate category

5. Write a detailed description including condition, age, and any defects

6. Set your price

7. Specify your preferred meeting location on campus

8. Review and publish your listing

The more details and better photos you provide, the more likely your item will sell quickly!

What items are prohibited from being sold?

The following items are prohibited on our platform:

- Illegal items or substances

- Alcohol, tobacco, or vaping products

- Prescription medications

- Weapons or weapon accessories

- Counterfeit or replica items

- Adult content or services

- Pets or animals

- Food items (unless commercially packaged and unopened)

- Items that violate university policies

Listings that violate these rules will be removed, and repeated violations may result in account suspension. See our Terms of Service for complete details.

How do I price my items?

Pricing your items appropriately will help them sell faster. Here are some tips:

- Research similar items on our platform to see the going rate

- For textbooks, check what percentage of the original price others are asking (typically 40-60% for good condition books)

- Consider the item's age, condition, and original price

- Factor in any accessories or extras you're including

- Price slightly higher than your minimum acceptable price to allow room for negotiation

- Be realistic - items with very high prices relative to their value tend to remain unsold

How can I stay safe when buying or selling?

Your safety is our top priority. Follow these guidelines:

- Keep all communication within our platform

- Meet in public, well-lit areas on campus during daylight hours

- Bring a friend to exchanges when possible

- Inspect items thoroughly before purchasing

- Use cash for transactions to avoid payment scams

- Trust your instincts - if something feels wrong, walk away

- Report suspicious users or activities to our team

- Never share personal financial information

For more detailed safety information, visit our Safety Tips page.

What should I do if I encounter a problem with another user?

If you experience issues with another user:

1. Try to resolve the issue directly through our messaging system first

2. If that doesn't work, use the "Report User" function on their profile

3. For serious concerns, contact our support team with details of the situation

4. In case of safety emergencies, contact campus security or local police

We take user reports seriously and investigate all complaints. Depending on the situation, we may mediate disputes, issue warnings, or suspend accounts that violate our community guidelines.

What payment methods should I use?

We recommend the following payment methods for in-person transactions:

- Cash is the most secure and immediate method

- Person-to-person payment apps (like Venmo, Cash App, or Zelle) are acceptable if both parties agree

- University payment systems if available on your campus

Avoid:

- Wire transfers

- Money orders or cashier's checks

- Payment requests from unknown apps

- Shipping items and accepting online payments

Remember to complete the payment only after inspecting the item in person.

Does the platform charge any fees?

No, our platform is completely free for university students to use. There are:

- No listing fees

- No transaction fees

- No membership fees

We believe in providing a free service to help students save money and reduce waste. All transactions are conducted directly between buyers and sellers, with no platform involvement in the payment process.
