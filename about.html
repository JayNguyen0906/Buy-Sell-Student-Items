About Campus Marketplace

Learn about our mission, values, and the team behind the platform

Our Story

Campus Marketplace was founded in 2020 by a group of university students who were frustrated with the existing options for buying and selling used items on campus. They wanted to create a platform specifically designed for the unique needs of college students.

What started as a small project at one university has now grown into a trusted marketplace serving students across dozens of campuses nationwide. Our platform helps students save money, reduce waste, and connect with their campus community.

Today, Campus Marketplace facilitates thousands of transactions each month, helping students find affordable textbooks, furniture, electronics, and more while providing a safe and convenient way to sell items they no longer need.

Our Mission & Values

At Campus Marketplace, our mission is to create a sustainable, affordable, and safe platform for university students to buy and sell used items within their campus community.

Safety First

We prioritize the safety of our users through verified accounts, secure meeting locations, and comprehensive safety guidelines.

Sustainability

By facilitating the reuse of items, we help reduce waste and promote sustainable consumption habits among students.

Affordability

We're committed to helping students save money by providing a fee-free platform to find affordable alternatives to new items.

Community

We foster connections between students on campus, creating a trusted network for exchanging goods and resources.

Trust & Transparency

Our platform is built on honest descriptions, fair pricing, and transparent policies for all users.

Student-Centered

Every feature and policy is designed with students' unique needs and circumstances in mind.
