Tips for Selling Textbooks

Take Clear Photos

Include multiple angles showing the cover, spine, and any wear or damage. Good photos increase buyer trust.

Price Fairly

Research market prices and consider the condition. A fair price helps your book sell faster.

Be Honest

Clearly describe any highlighting, notes, or damage. Honesty builds trust and prevents returns.

Respond Quickly

Reply to inquiries promptly. Fast communication increases your chances of making a sale.

Get Notified About New Textbooks

Sign up to receive alerts when new textbooks in your courses become available.
